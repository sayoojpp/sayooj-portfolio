About

I design where logic meets craft

I started in visual design and grew into product — which means I sweat the pixels and the problem. I care about the moment a confusing screen suddenly clicks, and I obsess over the small interactions that make software feel trustworthy.

My process is research-led but pragmatic: I prototype early, test with real users, and partner closely with engineering so designs actually ship. I've worked across healthcare, fintech, and SaaS, and I'm happiest turning operational complexity into something calm and human.
